## Best Interpretation

Use this distinction carefully:

- `discontinued` means Canon has actually ended the product
- `out of stock` means Canon is still listing it but you cannot buy it right now
- `feels discontinued` is the shopper reality when shelves stay empty for too long

For article writing:

- do not flatten those three ideas into one claim
- keep the answer date-specific
- say `as of May 3, 2026` when talking about current status
