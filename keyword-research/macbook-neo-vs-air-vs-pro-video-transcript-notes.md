- aimed at students, families, basic office use, and general everyday tasks

### MacBook Air

- positioned as the best balance in the lineup
- starting price claim: `$1,099`
- `M5` chip
- `16GB` memory and `512GB` storage standard
- stronger design, better ports, MagSafe, better webcam, more future-proof feel
- available in `13-inch` and `15-inch`

### MacBook Pro

- positioned as the performance and screen winner
- best display, best connectivity, best speakers, best sustained performance
- best fit for heavier creative or pro workflows
- transcript argues the sweet spot is usually not the cheapest Pro, but a stronger Pro configuration when buyers really need one
